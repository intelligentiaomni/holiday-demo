💡 Overview

This holiday season, our small hybrid AI lab is relocating, turning the move into an experiment in community support. We’re sharing selected materials from our early workspace. It's a mix of electronics, books, study items, and symbolic artifacts as tokens of appreciation to those who help sustain our open research and sandbox development.

All proceeds go directly to the lab’s ongoing development: hosting, tools, documentation, and collaborative prototyping. This is not a personal fundraiser; all funds are allocated toward lab activities and transparent research continuity.
